Fork2Farm – Community Supported Agriculture


  • (CSA) is a relatively recent and innovative concept that is establishing the relationship between Farmers and Consumers.
  • Consumers subscribe Farmer’s services, create demand in advance and In return, they receive a regular supply of sessional and selective farm produce.
  • Farmers grow only niche products as per demand so there will be always balance in production and pricing.
  • Subscription based On-Demand supply – Fresh farm produce that gives busy people convenient access to delicious, high quality, affordable, healthy food.
